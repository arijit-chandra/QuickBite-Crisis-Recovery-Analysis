# 🚀 QuickBite Crisis Recovery Analysis

### 📊 SQL + Power BI | Business Strategy & Consumer Analytics

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a **food delivery startup crisis scenario** and provides **data-driven recovery insights** using **SQL (SSMS)** and **Power BI (DAX + Visualization)**.

The goal was to evaluate the **impact of a major operational and reputational crisis (June 2025)** and recommend strategies to **recover customers, improve operations, and rebuild trust**.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem

QuickBite Express faced a severe crisis due to:

* Food safety violations (viral incident)
* Delivery outages during monsoon
* Increased competition pressure

### Key Challenges:

* 📉 Drop in daily orders
* 😡 Decline in customer satisfaction
* 🚫 High customer churn
* 🍽️ Restaurant partner attrition
* 💰 Rising customer acquisition costs

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL Server (SSMS)** → Data extraction, transformation, analysis
* **Power BI** → Dashboard & storytelling
* **DAX** → Measures, KPIs, calculated insights
* **Excel/CSV Handling** → Large dataset preprocessing

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Customer Segmentation

* Identified:

  * Recoverable customers
  * Churned high-value users
* Behavioral segmentation based on:

  * Order frequency
  * Ratings
  * Spending patterns

---

### 2️⃣ Order Trends Analysis

* Compared:

  * **Pre-crisis (Jan–May 2025)** vs **Crisis (Jun–Sep 2025)**
* Identified:

  * Sharp drop in order volume
  * City-level performance variations

---

### 3️⃣ Delivery Performance Insights

* Evaluated:

  * ⏱️ Average delivery time
  * ❌ Cancellation rates
  * 📦 SLA compliance

* Found:

  * Significant delays during crisis
  * SLA violations increased → impacted trust

---

### 4️⃣ Revenue Impact Analysis

* Calculated:

  * Revenue drop (subtotal + delivery fee – discount)
* Highlight:

  * Major decline driven by **reduced order volume + higher cancellations**

---

### 5️⃣ Customer Loyalty Impact

* Analyzed repeat customers (≥5 orders pre-crisis):

  * High churn among loyal users
  * Many churned users had **ratings > 4.5**

👉 Indicates: *Even satisfied customers left due to operational failure*

---

### 6️⃣ Restaurant Performance Analysis

* Identified:

  * Top-performing restaurants pre-crisis
  * Restaurants with highest decline

* Insight:

  * High-volume partners were also heavily impacted

---

### 7️⃣ Ratings & Sentiment Analysis

* Monthly rating trend tracking
* Word cloud for negative feedback

**Common negative keywords:**

* "late delivery"
* "cold food"
* "cancelled"
* "bad experience"

## 💡 Key Insights

* 🚨 Crisis caused **significant drop in orders and revenue**
* ⏱️ Delivery delays were the **biggest driver of dissatisfaction**
* 💔 Loyal customers churned despite high ratings
* 🌆 Certain cities were more impacted → location-based strategy needed
* 🍴 High-performing restaurants also saw major decline

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

### 🎯 Customer Recovery

* Target churned high-value users with:

  * Personalized offers
  * Apology campaigns

### 🚚 Operational Fixes

* Improve:

  * Delivery infrastructure
  * Real-time tracking
  * SLA adherence

### ⭐ Trust Rebuilding

* Highlight:

  * Food safety improvements
  * Verified restaurant badges

### 📣 Marketing Strategy

* Run **city-specific campaigns**
* Focus on **high-loss regions first**

### 🤝 Restaurant Retention

* Incentivize top partners
* Provide visibility boosts
